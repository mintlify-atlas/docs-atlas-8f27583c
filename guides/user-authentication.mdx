## Overview

Lynx Note provides a secure authentication system to protect your notes and personal data. This guide walks you through account registration, login, and account management workflows.

## Creating Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Registration">
    Click the "Sign Up" or "Register" button on the Lynx Note homepage to access the registration form.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Your Information">
    Provide the following required information:
    - **Name**: Your display name (will be shown to friends)
    - **Email**: A valid email address (must be unique)
    - **Password**: A secure password for your account
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Registration">
    Click the "Create Account" button. If the email is already registered, you'll receive an error message asking you to use a different email or log in instead.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Login After Registration">
    Once registered successfully, you'll be redirected to the login page with a confirmation message. You can now log in with your credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  All new accounts are automatically assigned a "user" role with access to all note-taking and collaboration features.
</Note>

## Logging In

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Login Page">
    Navigate to the Lynx Note login page. If you're already logged in, you'll be automatically redirected to the dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Provide your registered email address and password. Both fields are required.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Login">
    Click the "Log In" button. Upon successful authentication, you'll be welcomed back with a personalized greeting and redirected to your notes dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If you see an "Email or password is invalid" error, double-check that both your email and password are entered correctly. The system won't specify which field is incorrect for security reasons.
</Tip>

### Common Login Issues

**Empty Fields**
Both email and password fields must be filled out. Submitting with blank fields will display an error: "Username and password can't be blank."

**Invalid Credentials**
If your email or password doesn't match our records, you'll see an error message. Try resetting your password if you've forgotten it.

**Already Logged In**
If you're already authenticated, attempting to access the login page will automatically redirect you to the main dashboard.

## Managing Your Profile

Once logged in, you can view and update your account information:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Your Profile">
    Navigate to your user profile page from the main menu or navigation bar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Edit Information">
    Click the "Edit Profile" button to modify your:
    - Display name
    - Email address (must remain unique)
    - Password (leave blank to keep current password)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Submit your updates. If you're changing your email to one that's already registered, you'll receive an error and need to choose a different email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  When updating your profile, if you leave the password field blank, your existing password will remain unchanged. Only fill in the password fields if you want to change it.
</Note>

## Logging Out

To end your session securely:

1. Click the "Log Out" or "Sign Out" button in the navigation menu
2. Your session will be terminated immediately
3. You'll be redirected to the homepage with a friendly goodbye message
4. You'll need to log in again to access your notes

<Tip>
  Always log out when using Lynx Note on shared or public computers to protect your account and notes from unauthorized access.
</Tip>

## Deleting Your Account

If you need to permanently delete your Lynx Note account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Account Settings">
    Navigate to your profile or account settings page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Look for the "Delete Account" option and confirm your decision. This action cannot be undone.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automatic Logout">
    Your account and all associated data will be deleted, and you'll be automatically logged out.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Deleting your account is permanent and will remove all your notes, collections, and friendships. Make sure to export any important data before proceeding.
</Note>

## Security Best Practices

- Use a strong, unique password for your Lynx Note account
- Don't share your login credentials with others
- Log out from shared devices after each session
- Update your password periodically for enhanced security
- Keep your email address current to receive important account notifications
